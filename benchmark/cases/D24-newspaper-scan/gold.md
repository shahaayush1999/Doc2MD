# The Harbor Chronicle
Edition: March 14, 1932
Headline: Ferry Service Restored After Winter Storm
Subhead: Crews cleared the east pier before dawn.
Column 1: The first ferry departed at 6:45 in the morning with twenty-seven passengers.
Column 2: Dockmaster Elias Ward said the fog signal would remain active until Friday.
Caption: Workers inspect the repaired gangway beside Pier 3.
